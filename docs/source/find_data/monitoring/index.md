See also  **DTRA Wavefroms from Nuclear Explosions (WFNE)**.
The WFNE consists of source information (date/time, location, yield, seismic magnitude, burial depth, etc.), drawn from a variety of official and unofficial sources, and a collection of related digital waveform data for all nuclear explosions conducted from 1945-2017. The WFNE website includes 2,157 announced or presumed nuclear explosions, 1,113 of which have 148,912 digital waveforms available from WFNE (in CSS and SAC formats), recorded at 394 stations distributed worldwide. [website](https://www.wfne.info/)
